  3. All your friends with two clicks

    Typing out whole email addresses is so 2005! With Postman, simply connect via Facebook the first time you launch the app and instantly search through all your friends, send them links and receive them too.

  4. A truly personal inbox

    No one knows you better than your friends, so who better to send you links to the things you want to see?

    Whether it’s work-related, a new music video or just a tweet; Postman delivers instant desktop notifications and stores your links in your own personal inbox for you to check in your own time.
